Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Overview

This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which bookings with Discover Nepal Travel may be cancelled and the associated charges. By making a booking, you agree to this policy.

Important: We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that includes trip cancellation coverage at the time of booking.

2. Standard Cancellation Charges

The following cancellation charges apply to all standard bookings:

Cancellation Timeline & Charges

When You Cancel Cancellation Charge Refund Amount
More than 60 days before departure Deposit (30% of total) 70% of total cost
30-59 days before departure 50% of total cost 50% of total cost
15-29 days before departure 75% of total cost 25% of total cost
Less than 15 days before departure 100% of total cost No refund
After trip commencement or no-show 100% of total cost No refund

Calculation Example:

For a trip costing $1,000:

  • Cancellation 45 days before departure: You receive $500 refund (50%)
  • Cancellation 20 days before departure: You receive $250 refund (25%)
  • Cancellation 10 days before departure: No refund

Note: Cancellation date is calculated from the trip start date, not the booking date.

3. Special Cancellation Policies

Flight-Inclusive Packages

For packages including domestic flights (e.g., Lukla, Jomsom):

  • Flight tickets are non-refundable once issued
  • Additional airline cancellation charges apply
  • Name changes on tickets may incur fees

Restricted Area Treks

For restricted area permits (Upper Mustang, Manaslu, etc.):

  • Permit fees are non-refundable once approved
  • 100% cancellation charge within 30 days
  • Special permit regulations apply

Group Bookings

For groups of 6+ people:

  • Deposit: 50% of total cost
  • Balance due: 60 days before departure
  • Cancellation charges start at 60 days

Luxury Packages

For luxury/honeymoon packages:

  • Deposit: 50% of total cost
  • Non-refundable within 45 days
  • Special hotel cancellation policies apply

4. Date Changes & Modifications

Date Changes

Change to alternative date more than 30 days before departure: $50 administration fee + any cost differences.

Participant Changes

Transfer booking to another person more than 30 days before departure: $75 administration fee.

Itinerary Changes

Major itinerary changes requested by client may be treated as cancellation and rebooking.

Last-Minute Changes

Changes within 30 days of departure subject to cancellation charges as per section 2.

5. Cancellation by Discover Nepal Travel

We reserve the right to cancel a trip due to:

Insufficient Bookings

If minimum group size not met 30 days before departure. We will notify you and offer:

  • Alternative departure date
  • Alternative trip of similar value
  • Full refund of all payments

Force Majeure

Events beyond our control including:

  • Natural disasters
  • Political unrest
  • Pandemics
  • Government restrictions

Credit note issued for future travel.

Safety Concerns

Based on official advisories or ground conditions. Options include:

  • Modified itinerary
  • Future travel credit
  • Partial/full refund

6. Refund Process

Refund Timeline & Method

  1. Cancellation Request: Submit in writing via email
  2. Processing Time: 7-14 business days
  3. Refund Method: Original payment method preferred
  4. Bank Charges: Any bank/transfer fees deducted from refund
  5. Currency: Refunds in original currency of payment

Note: Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement after processing.

7. Insurance Claims

If you cancel for reasons covered by your travel insurance:

  • We will provide necessary documentation for your claim
  • Refund will be issued directly to you, not your insurer
  • Claim processing is between you and your insurance provider

Travel Insurance Requirement

Mandatory: All clients must have comprehensive travel insurance covering trip cancellation, medical expenses, and emergency evacuation. We cannot process insurance claims on your behalf.

8. COVID-19 & Pandemic Policy

Special Pandemic Provisions

In the event of pandemic-related restrictions:

  • Government Travel Ban: Full credit for future travel (valid 24 months)
  • Positive Test Before Departure: Medical certificate required for future travel credit
  • Quarantine Requirements: Additional costs borne by client
  • Vaccination Requirements: Client responsibility to comply

Flexible Booking: For peace of mind, consider our flexible booking option (additional cost applies).

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I get sick during the trek?

A: No refunds for unused services or early departure. This is why medical and trip interruption insurance is mandatory.

Q: Can I transfer my booking to next year?

A: Yes, more than 30 days before departure with $50 fee + any price differences. Within 30 days, cancellation charges apply.

Q: What if my flight to Nepal is cancelled?

A: Contact your airline and insurance provider. We cannot refund for missed international flights.

Q: Are there any non-refundable costs?

A: Yes, including but not limited to: issued flight tickets, permit fees, non-refundable hotel deposits.

11. Policy Updates

This cancellation policy may be updated periodically. The version applicable to your booking is the one in effect at the time of booking. Always check our website for the most current policy.
