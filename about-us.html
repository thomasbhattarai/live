Our Story

From local passion to professional travel service

Founded by Mountain Enthusiasts

Discover Nepal Travel was founded in 2010 by a group of experienced trekking guides and travel enthusiasts who shared a common passion: showcasing the authentic beauty of Nepal to the world. What began as a small family-run operation in Thamel, Kathmandu, has grown into a reputable travel agency trusted by thousands of international travelers.

Our founders, born and raised in the Himalayas, combined their deep local knowledge with professional tourism training to create experiences that go beyond typical tourist itineraries. We believe in creating meaningful connections between travelers and Nepal's rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm-hearted people.

Over the past decade, we've proudly organized trips for over 15,000 travelers from 60+ countries, maintained a 98% customer satisfaction rate, and received multiple awards for sustainable tourism practices.
